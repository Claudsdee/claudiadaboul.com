Claudia Daboul

Junior UX Designer building Quality & Empathy into every step

· About Me · Case Studies · Contact Me · CV ·

A problem solver who loves continuous improvement, Claudia has never shied away from wanting to make things better for everyone.

Feeling like she could apply herself more to improving things, Claudia has started off the process of improving herself by constantly unlearning and learning new ways of doing. Her first step; to break into human centred design and become a UX Designer. She believes that the UX methodologies that she has acquired in her tool kit, such as researching, user personas and flows, prototyping and usability testing to name a few, can be applied to any problem to come up with a solution.

Claudia is someone who enjoys bright colours, post-it notes and scrapbooking and even dabbles in DJ-ing, she would like to expand her creative consciousness and create solutions.
